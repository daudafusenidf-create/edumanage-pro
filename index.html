Promotion Guide

GES class progression order:

Creche→ Nursery 1→ Nursery 2→ KG1→ KG2→ BS.1→ BS.2→ BS.3→ BS.4→ BS.5→ BS.7→ BS.8→ BS.9
Safe promotion: Only student.class changes. Reports, fees and attendance are linked to year + term, not to class — so nothing is lost.
